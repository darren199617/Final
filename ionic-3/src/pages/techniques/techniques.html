1. Numbers

Below there are some methods for trying to remember numbers such as phone numbers

When looking at numbers there is two different ways to try remember them. The first is to check if the number has something memorable. An example of theis would be: 19162000 the above number could be broken up into two separate parts:

1916[Easter Rising], 2000[Millenium]

19162000

Another way of remembering numbers is to break them up into smaller sections that may not normally be memorable. A phone number becomes far easier to learn once it has been divided. An example of this would be: 1800456987 The above number would be easier to learn if broken into three sections:

1800[1] 456[2] 987[3]

1800456987

2. People

Below are some ways to remember names

To try remember someone's name you should try the following tips:

1.When meeting someone for thefirst time you should repeat their name out loud.

For Example: Aften being introduced to Stephen you could ask. "So stephen, what do you work as?".

2.You could try to associate the person with a place or a job. so when Stephen answers your question, try to associate his answer with his name

"Stephen the carpenter"

3.Repeat their name silently to yourself.
