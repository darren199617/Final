Below are some tips to help you get through day to day life

1) Use a calendar at home.

You should get a large wall calendar that you chack every single morning. The calendar should contain all important events. Write all appointmennts in this calendar. Dont forget to copy everything at the start of each new year.

2) Use a Notebook

A notebook is portable so you can bring it with you, so that you dont forget to do things. You should try and get into the habit of using the notebook. You can also have a section to reference phone numbers and addresses.

3) Managing medication

The best way to manage your medication if you suffer from meory probelms is to use a pillbox. You should use a weekly pillbox. This ensures that you dont double-dose and you dont forget to take them. You should also associate taking medication with a daily task. You could try to get into the habit of taking medication every morning when you brush your teeth.

When taking medicationion you should stop for a second and think "Im taking Friday's medication". This means it is more likely for you to remember taking the medication.

4)External Memory Aids

There are many things you can use in day-to-day life that will make it easier. Below are some examples.

Shopping lists- so you dont forget what you need to buy.

Kitchen timer- Alarms are useful reminders so that you do not forget about what you are cooking.

Your Hand - If you need to remember something important, that you think you may forget you should write it on your hand.
